Website Planning Document: Target Audience


Target Audience


The target audience for "Aberdeen Chamber" is as follows:

  • Who: People interested in visiting the lovely city of Aberdeen, located in Washington State. Also, young married couples , youth, active single young adults, senior citizens, and families in general who live in the area.
  • Age: 13 and older.
  • Technology: The majority of the audience will access the site via a mobile device like a Cell phone or tablet. The minority will access the site with a desktop/laptop computer.
  • Income: Income types will vary according to age and levels of education (to a medium of $65,000 and above annually).
  • Motivation: Wanting to connect with the community at large by:
    1. Investigating or joining the business community
    2. Wanting to participate with local community events put on by the local businesses.
