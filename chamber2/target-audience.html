Website Planning Document: Target Audience


Target Audience


The target audience for "Aberdeen Chamber" is as follows:

  • Who: People interested in visiting the lovely city of Aberdeen, located in Washington State. Also, young married couples , youth, active single young adults, senior citizens, and families in general who live in the area.
  • Age: 13 and older.
  • Technology: The majority of the audience will access the site via a mobile device like a Cell phone or tablet. The minority will access the site with a desktop/laptop computer.
  • Income: Income types will vary according to age and levels of education (to a medium of $65,000 and above annually).
  • Motivation: Wanting to connect with the community at large by:
    1. Investigating or joining the business community
    2. Wanting to participate with local community events put on by the local businesses.

Personas

Henry R Jones Jr. & Family

  • Henry is a newly "work from home" professional from the Seattle area. Being a young father and feeling unsafe in his previous apartment in Seattle. Decided to Move to Aberdeen where housing is affordable and the streets are much safer and cleaner than the big city of Seattle. Wanting to become a part of the community he wants to get the best information available.

Henry R Jones Sr. & Wife

  • Henry Sr. Recently retired and has lived in teh Big city almost his whole life. Wanting to be close to their grandkids they decide to move to Aberdeen for similar reasons. By moving to a small town close to the ocean he always wanted to open an antique shop with his wife. With the redicilious amount of money they sold their Downtown home for they were able to purchase their home outright and the buildign that they will be setting up shop in for their store. They want connect with the local community and businesses to establish their store as the premier antique store in the Harbor.
